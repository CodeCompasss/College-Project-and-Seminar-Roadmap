## ðŸ’¡ The Lessons Learned

!!! success "The Paper Trail Saved Me"
    > *"I sent my guide an update email every single week for 6 months. At the end, when my teammate tried to claim he did more work than me, I forwarded all 24 emails to the HOD. Case closed."*

!!! success "The 'I Fixed It' Power Move"
    > *"During my Phase 1 review, my guide ripped my synopsis apart. I went home, fixed everything, and showed up at his cabin at 9 AM the next day with the corrected version. He smiled and said, 'This is how engineers work.' I got extra marks."*

!!! success "The Backup Video Saved My Life"
    > *"My app crashed during the live demo. The examiner looked annoyed. I said, 'Sir, I have a recorded video of the full demo.' I played a 3-minute video. He nodded and said, 'Good preparation.' I passed."*
